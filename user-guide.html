User Guide

Follow these steps to track your bonuses with confidence.

  1. Create an account: Use an email to sign up in the prototype.
  2. Add an offer: Enter the bank name, bonus amount, and the rules.
  3. Define tasks: Add deposits, card swipes, or balance holds with due dates.
  4. Check progress: Mark tasks done and verify status weekly.
  5. Confirm payout: Record the payout date to update analytics.

Tips

  • Check the dashboard once a week to avoid last minute rushes
  • Keep notes for each offer to track special rules
  • Archive completed offers to keep the list focused
