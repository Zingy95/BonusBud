User Guide

This guide walks you through setting up BonusBud, tracking offers, and collecting payouts without missing deadlines.

Quick Start

1) Create Your Account

Sign up to get your personal dashboard. Confirm your email to enable reminders.

2) Add Your First Offer

Open the Offer Catalog and select a bank signup bonus to track. Review eligibility before adding.

3) Follow the Checklist

Complete tasks like direct deposit, debit transactions, or maintaining a minimum balance.

4) Turn On Reminders

Enable notifications for task deadlines and payout windows so you never miss a step.

5) Mark as Completed

When you finish tasks, mark them done. Log the payout date and amount.

6) Track Your Results

Use the Analytics Dashboard to see total bonuses earned and your completion rate.

Feature Details

  • Offer Catalog: Key payout, requirements, eligibility notes, and timing estimates.
  • Rule Engine & Checklist: Auto-generated tasks tailored to each offer’s terms.
  • Deadline Tracker: Reminder schedule with suggested “safe” buffer dates.
  • Notes & Attachments: Add your own notes or upload confirmation images (e.g., approval emails).
  • Analytics Dashboard: Lifetime earnings, active offers, and historical completions.

FAQs

Do I need to link bank accounts?
No. BonusBud is privacy-first and uses user-entered data only.

Can I track multiple offers at once?
Yes. Track as many active offers as you want (limits may apply in the free tier).

What if a bank changes its terms?
Update the task list manually in your offer entry. We recommend adding a note with the date and source.

How do reminders work?
You’ll get alerts before each task or payout deadline based on your start date and the offer rules you entered.

Troubleshooting

  • Not receiving reminders: Check your email address/notification permissions and re-save your settings.
  • Offer not appearing: Refresh the page or re-add the offer; confirm you saved it to your dashboard.
  • Checklist seems wrong: Verify the bank’s current terms and edit the tasks to match.
  • Missed a deadline: Add a note and adjust your reminder lead time (e.g., 7–10 days earlier next time).
Accessibility & Privacy
BonusBud aims for keyboard navigation and readable contrast. We never require bank logins; all data is user-entered.
