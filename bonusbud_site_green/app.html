How BonusBud Works

BonusBud is a web and Android based application designed to help you find, track, and complete bank signup bonuses. It organizes every offer in one place so you never miss a payout again.

Offer Catalog

Browse a list of current bank signup bonuses with key payout details and eligibility requirements.

Rule Engine and Checklist

Track specific requirements like direct deposits, debit card purchases, or maintaining a minimum balance.

Deadline Tracker

Get automated reminders and alerts before any bonus requirement or payout deadline expires.

Analytics Dashboard

View your total bonuses earned, completion rates, and earning history at a glance.

Technical Notes

  • Web and Android compatible
  • User entered data only (no sensitive bank logins)
  • Simple, secure, and privacy focused
